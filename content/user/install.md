On the device:

1. Launch "Install Apps".
1. Select your Wi-Fi network name from the list. If it's not on the list, open the menu and select "restart app" to refresh the list.
1. Enter the Wi-Fi password and press "OK".
1. Wait for the device to connect to the Wi-Fi and server. If it fails to connect, press `W` to go back and check if you had selected the correct Wi-Fi network and entered the correct password.
1. The "Session ID" (8 digits) should appear. You'll need it.

On the computer (or phone):

1. Download the app from [catalog.fireflyzero.com](https://catalog.fireflyzero.com/)
1. Go to [install.fireflyzero.com](https://install.fireflyzero.com/).
1. Enter the Session ID from the device.
1. Select the file.
1. Press "install".

Now the device will download and install the app. If all is good, you can launch and play it rightaway!
